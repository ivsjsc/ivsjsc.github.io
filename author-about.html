About the Author

NMT - The Storyteller of Cosmic Balances

NMT, a pen name, is the creative force behind the epic saga LEGNAXE, a tale that weaves together the celestial struggles of angels, the complexities of human emotions, and the eternal quest for balance in a universe teetering on the edge of chaos.

Based in Vietnam, NMT draws inspiration from a rich tapestry of mythology, philosophy, and personal experiences to craft stories that resonate with readers across cultures.

Journey to LEGNAXE

The creation of LEGNAXE began as a personal exploration of the myths and legends that have shaped human understanding of the divine. NMT was particularly fascinated by the concept of angels not as infallible beings, but as entities capable of doubt, rebellion, and growth.

This fascination, combined with a love for storytelling, led to the birth of Healiangel, Lucifer, and the sprawling cosmic narrative that spans Heaven, Earth, and beyond.

Beyond the Pages

When not writing, NMT is an avid reader, a lover of nature, and a keen observer of human behavior. They find inspiration in the quiet moments of life—whether it’s the rustle of leaves in a rural Vietnamese village or the vibrant chaos of a city street.

NMT also enjoys engaging with readers, believing that stories are a shared journey between writer and audience.

Thank you for joining NMT on this cosmic adventure. As Healiangel would say, "Balance is not the absence of conflict, but the courage to embrace both light and shadow."
