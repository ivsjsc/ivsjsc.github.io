Key Considerations for Prospective Students

  • English Proficiency

    Most programs require IELTS (5.5-6.5) or TOEFL. Some offer preparatory courses.

  • Academic Requirements

    A relevant Bachelor's degree is standard. GPA and work experience requirements vary.

  • Accreditation

    Look for international accreditations (AACSB, ABET, AUN-QA, etc.) for quality assurance.

  • Degree-Granting Body

    Understand if the degree is from a Vietnamese university, a foreign partner, or dual-awarded.

  • Tuition & Living Costs

    Fees vary significantly. HCMC living costs are generally affordable.

  • Visa Requirements

    Understand the student visa process for Vietnam.

  • Verify Information

    Crucial: Always contact universities directly for the most current program details, fees, and deadlines.
