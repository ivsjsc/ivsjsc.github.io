Career Opportunities for Foreign Personnel

Join Our Dynamic International Team at IVS Education in Vietnam

Grow Your Career with IVS JSC

IVS Academy, a pioneer in international education under IVS JSC, invites passionate and qualified professionals from around the globe to join our growing team in Vietnam. We are dedicated to empowering students through innovative English programs, international collaborations, and cutting-edge educational technology. Discover your path with us!

Strategic Partners

We collaborate with globally recognized organizations to deliver exceptional educational experiences. Opportunities exist for professionals experienced in international relations, partnership management, and program development.

  • Work with international teams across Asia (Philippines, Taiwan, etc.).
  • Manage and expand partnerships with educational institutions.
  • Contribute to the design and implementation of cross-border programs.
  • Requirements: Proven experience in international relations or partnership management, strong communication skills, fluency in English (additional languages are a plus).

Senior Staff & Business Management

Drive our vision forward in leadership and management roles overseeing operations, strategic planning, program quality, business development, and marketing.

Senior Leadership Positions:

  • Oversee academic quality, program development, and operational efficiency.
  • Lead initiatives like the International Summer Camp.
  • Example Role: Academic Director, Program Director.
  • Requirements: 5+ years in education management, strong leadership skills, relevant qualifications, fluency in English.

Business Managers:

  • Develop strategic partnerships, manage marketing campaigns, oversee budgets.
  • Expand market reach in Southeast Asia, negotiate contracts.
  • Example Role: Business Development Manager, Marketing Manager.
  • Requirements: 3+ years in business development/marketing/sales (education sector preferred), strong negotiation skills, fluency in English.

International Connections & Coordination

Our global network offers unique career experiences in managing international projects and collaborations.

  • Liaise with overseas partners (Philippines, Taiwan, USA, Europe).
  • Manage logistics for international programs (visas, travel, accommodation).
  • Support cross-cultural exchange programs for students and teachers.
  • Design and execute international initiatives like summer camps and study tours.
  • Example Role: International Coordinator, Project Specialist.
  • Requirements: Experience in project management or international coordination, excellent organizational skills, cultural sensitivity, fluency in English.
  • Benefits: Potential for international travel, global networking.

Highly Qualified Teachers & Trainers

Shape the future of our students by delivering engaging and effective lessons.

Native English Teachers (NETs):

  • Deliver high-quality 1:1 and group ESL classes (focus on 4 skills) for various age groups (Kids, Juniors, Adults).
  • Utilize modern teaching methodologies and technologies.
  • Contribute to curriculum development and student assessment.
  • Requirements: Native English speaker, Bachelor's degree, TEFL/TESOL/CELTA certification, 2+ years of teaching experience (experience in Asia preferred), passion for teaching.

Specialized Trainers (STEAM, Sports):

  • Lead engaging STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Math) workshops and projects.
  • Coach sports activities (e.g., swimming, basketball, badminton).
  • Example Role: STEAM Instructor, Sports Coach.
  • Requirements: Relevant expertise and qualifications in the specific field (e.g., STEM background, coaching certifications), experience working with youth, adaptability, enthusiasm.

General Teacher Benefits:

  • Competitive salary package based on qualifications and experience.
  • Work permit and visa support.
  • Accommodation support/allowance (details provided during interview).
  • Professional development opportunities.
  • Supportive and collaborative international working environment.
